ABOUT

A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

Our mission is to help you be successful in your health and wellbeing by offering a supportive, encouraging environment with state-of-the-art equipment so you can experience a higher quality of life. We know you want to triumph in everything you do, and we want to be part of your journey to victory.

OUR HISTORY



Main Street Gym & Fitness was originally founded in 2004 by Scott Biskie. After he retired, he sold the gym to two of his Tae Kwon Do Students; Isaac North and Trevor Steinert. Both Trevor and Isaac work to establish a family-oriented atmosphere that allows everyone to feel comfortable while getting a good workout.
