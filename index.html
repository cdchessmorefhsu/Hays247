Hays Main Street Gym

We are a 24 hour, 7 days a week gym and fitness facility with a sauna, tanning bed, and certified training staff.

FEATURES

  • 24/7 Access

    Members get 24 hour, 7 days a week access to the gym.

  • Cardio Equipment

    Durable stationary bikes, treadmills, a Woodway Curve treadmill, and a Nexersys machine for kickboxing and MMA training.

  • Weight Room

    Large weight room with a variety of options including free weights, olympic platforms & plates, and weight machines.

  • Amenities

    Our gym offers a sauna and tanning booth to gym members so you can look and feel your best.

  • Trained Staff

    We offer a certified personal trainer and nutritionist on staff to help you reach your goals.

  • Membership Programs

    Over a dozen membership programs; including 'Thank You' discounts to medical professionals, military, and more!
